I love messing around with hardware, spatial computing, and robotics. If it involves making pixels interactive or machines smarter, I'm probably building it. Most of my work sits at the intersection of industrial hardware (like PLCs and Dobot robotic arms) and real-time computer vision.

> **Did you know?** You can map a gyroscopic sensor's coordinates to synthesize a functional electronic guitar. Check out the math behind it in my [Air-Guitar Project](https://github.com/RoboX2020/Air-Guitar) 🎸
